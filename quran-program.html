Comprehensive Quran Education

Our Quran program develops students' relationship with the Holy Quran through memorization (Hifz), proper recitation (Tajweed), and understanding (Tafseer) at all grade levels.

Daily Quran classes for all students
Optional full Hifz program
Certified Quran teachers
Annual Quran competitions

Program Structure

Age-appropriate Quran learning progression

Early Years

KG & Nursery

  • Short surah memorization
  • Basic tajweed rules
  • Islamic songs and nasheeds
  • Daily Quran exposure

Primary School

Grades 1-6

  • Juz Amma completion
  • Applied tajweed rules
  • Basic tafseer lessons
  • Daily memorization

Secondary School

JSS/SSS

  • Selected surah memorization
  • Advanced tajweed
  • Detailed tafseer studies
  • Hifz program option

Full Hifz Program
