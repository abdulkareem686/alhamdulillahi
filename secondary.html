Comprehensive Islamic Secondary Education

Our Secondary School program prepares students for higher education and life as practicing Muslims through rigorous academics, Islamic scholarship, and character development.

Junior Secondary

JSS 1 - JSS 3

Broad-based curriculum with core Islamic studies and introduction to specialization

Senior Secondary

SSS 1 - SSS 3

Specialization in sciences, arts, or commercial fields with advanced Islamic studies

Secondary Curriculum

Balancing academic excellence and Islamic values

Junior Secondary (JSS 1-3)

Core Subjects:

  • Quran Studies (Memorization & Tafseer)
  • Arabic Language
  • Islamic Studies (Aqeedah, Fiqh, Seerah)
  • English, Mathematics, Basic Science
  • Social Studies, Computer Science

Electives:

  • Islamic Art and Calligraphy
  • Quranic Sciences
  • Introductory Business Studies

Senior Secondary (SSS 1-3)

Core Subjects:

  • Advanced Quran Studies
  • Arabic Literature
  • Islamic Jurisprudence
  • English, Mathematics
  • Civic Education

Specializations:

Science Track

Physics, Chemistry, Biology

Arts Track

Literature, History, Islamic Arts

Commercial Track

Accounting, Business Studies, Economics

Academic Clubs

  • Debate Society

  • Mathematics Circle

College Preparation

  • Standardized test preparation
  • Career guidance
  • Leadership training
