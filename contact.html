Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from parents

Our regular school hours are from 7:30 AM to 3:30 PM, Monday through Friday. Students should arrive by 7:15 AM for morning assembly. Early drop-off and after-school care options are available for an additional fee.

We currently do not provide school buses, but many families organize carpooling. We can connect you with other families in your area to arrange transportation. Some local transportation services also offer routes to our school.

We maintain an average student-to-teacher ratio of 15:1 to ensure personalized attention for each student. In some specialized classes and Islamic studies, the ratio may be even smaller to facilitate better learning.

Islamic values are integrated throughout our curriculum and daily activities. Students begin the day with Quran recitation and dua, perform daily prayers together, learn Islamic etiquette, and study Islamic subjects. Our teachers model Islamic behavior and incorporate Islamic perspectives in all subjects.

We offer a variety of extracurricular activities including Quran competition teams, Islamic arts, sports, science club, community service projects, and more. Activities vary by season and student interest, with new options added regularly based on student and parent feedback.
