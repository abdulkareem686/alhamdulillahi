Early Years Program

Nurturing Young Muslims Ages 3-7

Islamic Foundation for Young Learners

Our Early Years program provides a nurturing Islamic environment where children ages 3-7 develop spiritually, socially, and academically through play-based learning and Quranic teachings.

Daily Quran memorization (short surahs and duas)
Islamic manners and etiquette training
Play-based academic foundation
Qualified early childhood educators

Our Early Years Classes

Age-appropriate Islamic and academic development

KG 1

Ages 3-4

  • Arabic alphabet introduction
  • Basic Islamic manners
  • Short surah memorization
  • Fine motor skills development

KG 2

Ages 4-5

  • Quranic letter recognition
  • Daily Islamic routines
  • Memorization of Juz Amma surahs
  • Early math and literacy

Nursery 1

Ages 5-6

  • Basic Quran reading
  • Islamic stories and seerah
  • Full salah memorization
  • Reading and writing readiness

Nursery 2

Ages 6-7

  • Quran reading foundation
  • Islamic dua memorization
  • Practical salah practice
  • Primary school preparation
