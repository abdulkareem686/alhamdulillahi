Comprehensive Islamic & Academic Education

At Al Hamdulillah Academy, we provide a balanced curriculum that combines national educational standards with comprehensive Islamic studies.

Our academic program is designed to develop critical thinking, creativity, and problem-solving skills while instilling strong Islamic values and character. We follow the national curriculum enhanced with Islamic perspectives across all subjects.

The Islamic studies program includes Quran memorization (Hifz), Arabic language, Islamic history, Aqeedah, Fiqh, and Seerah of the Prophet Muhammad (PBUH). Our students also participate in daily prayers and weekly Islamic activities.

Hifz Program

Our Hifz program is designed for students who wish to memorize the entire Quran while continuing their academic education. This rigorous program requires dedication and commitment from both students and parents.

Program Structure:
  • Daily memorization sessions with qualified Huffaz
  • Regular revision schedule to retain memorized portions
  • Tajweed and proper recitation training
  • Understanding of memorized verses (short Tafseer)
  • Continued academic studies at modified pace
Admission Requirements:
Strong Memorization Ability

Demonstrated capacity for memorization and retention.

Good Quranic Recitation

Ability to recite Quran with basic Tajweed rules.

Parental Commitment

Parents must support the rigorous schedule and home revision.

Interview & Assessment

Evaluation by our Hifz committee for suitability.
