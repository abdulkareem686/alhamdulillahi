Our Story

Established in 2010, Al Hamdulillah Academy has grown from a small Islamic learning center to a full-fledged educational institution serving students from KG to Secondary levels.

Islamic Environment

Daily prayers, Quran classes, and Islamic character development

Academic Excellence

National curriculum with enhanced Islamic studies

Qualified Staff

Certified teachers in both secular and Islamic subjects

Our History

Founded in 2010 with just 25 students, we've grown to serve over 500 students across all grade levels while maintaining our commitment to Islamic values.

Our Approach

We combine academic rigor with Islamic teachings to develop well-rounded Muslim students prepared for both worldly success and the hereafter.

Our Growth

From our humble beginnings, we've expanded our facilities, curriculum, and staff to meet the growing demand for quality Islamic education.
