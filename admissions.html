Admission Process

How to enroll your child at Al Hamdulillah Academy

1

Inquiry

Submit an inquiry form or contact our admissions office to learn more about our programs.

2

School Tour

Schedule a visit to tour our facilities and meet with our staff.

3

Application

Complete the online application form and submit required documents.

4

Assessment

Student assessment and family interview with administration.

5

Acceptance

Receive admission decision and enrollment package.

6

Registration

Complete registration forms and submit tuition deposit.

7

Orientation

Attend new student and parent orientation session.

8

First Day

Begin your journey at Al Hamdulillah Academy!

Admission Requirements

What you need to prepare for application

Completed Application Form

All sections must be filled out completely and signed by parents/guardians.

Birth Certificate

Official copy showing child's date of birth.

Medical Records

Immunization records and recent physical examination.

Academic Records

Report cards and transcripts from previous schools (if applicable).

Passport Photos

Two recent passport-sized photographs of the student.

Frequently Asked Questions

Yes, we accept mid-year transfers if space is available in the desired grade. The admission process is the same, and we work closely with transferring students to help them adjust to our curriculum and environment.
